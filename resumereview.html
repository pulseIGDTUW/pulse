Good Resume Tips

  • Keep it concise and to the point.
  • Keep it a single page long.
  • Highlight your key achievements.
  • Use bullet points for clarity.
  • Avoid putting a photo.
  • Avoid using multiple colors.
  • Proofread for errors and typos.
